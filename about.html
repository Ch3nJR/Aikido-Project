aikiForest is a team of Aikido teachers from diverse backgrounds offering different talents, skills and experiences. We develop and deliver high quality and learner-centric Aikido programmes to our students.

aikiForest's vision is to be the educational powerhouse of contemporary Aikido. We are confident that with the team we have gathered, the teachers and the students will work together to explore limits and boundaries, where we can collectively achieve our bold goal.
