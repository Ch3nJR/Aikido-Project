Questions on your mind?

Comparing Aikido with other martial arts like Taekwondo, Aikido is a more defensive styled martial arts which focuses on neutralising your opponent in a peaceful and harmonious manner without resulting in violence.

In aikiForest, we focus on teaching aikido while focusing on core fundamentals based on groups. Age 4-6 will be on posture and balance. Age 7-15 will be on skills and coordination. Age 16 and above will be control and confidence.

All class schedule of the 3 different outlets can be found here. Duration of all classes is 1hr.

Class fees are collected every quarterly (every 3 months). If either you or your child join midway, the class fee will be pro-rated for that month.

Make up lessons are available to suit a schedule of your choice. A maximum number of 4 make up lessons can be carried forward to the next term.

No pre-requisite at all! Just wear comfortable clothing and long pants on that day and you are all set to go.
