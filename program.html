Enrichments & Workshops

Preschool Seed Programme

Age 4-6 (N2-K2 Level)

This programme partake in a term thematic approach which focuses on learning fundamentals through activities & games. It also includes home-based revision videos, worksheets to reinforce understanding as well as assessment & upgrading to reflect child's progress. This programme can also cater for integrating classroom showcase or school performance.

PAL: Programme for Active Learning

Age 7-8 (P1-P2 Level)

This programme consists of 7-10 modules of sports & games. You can select from 3 different domains: Mind Cultivation, Physical Conditioning or Life Skills. The main objective of this programme is to focus on social emotional learning.

CCA: Co-Curricular Activity

Age 7-12 (P1-P6 Level)

This programme is conducted as a regular on-going class where students will be graded as they aim towards obtaining different coloured belts. The main objective of this focuses on the acquisition of basic techniques

School Holiday Activity

Age 5-12 (K1 & K2, P1-P6 Level)

This programme has various exciting themes available for each school holiday. All you need to do is select the required number of sessions and we will take it from there. The main objective of this is to focus on game-based learning.

School Excursion Venue

Age 5-12 (K1 & K2, P1-P6 Level)

This programme is conducted as an excursion to an Aikido Dojo (Training Centre). We hope that this will allow the students to have a greater appreciation of the Japanese Culture & Etiquettes. The main objective of this is to focus on experiential learning.

Themed Introductory Workshop

Age: Adults - Staffs, Teachers, Parents, Family, Public etc

Select the type of theme for example, self-defence, life-skills, team-building etc. This introductory workshop is simple, easy & hands-on, all while focusing on the appreciation of a new Art.

Aikido Demonstration or Talk

Age: Adults - Staffs, Teachers, Parents, Family, Public etc

Instead of a workshop, you can choose to have a demonstration or a talk. This provides an engaging showcase of what Aikido is all about while focusing on understanding a new perspective.
